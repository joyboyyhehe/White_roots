❓ FAQ

Questions Parents
Often Ask

What is the admission age cutoff? ⌄
Children must be 2 years old by June 1, 2025 for Sprouting Seeds; 3 years for Little Saplings; and 4 years for Budding Branches. We do consider children who are close to the age on a case-by-case basis.
Do you offer a trial class before enrollment? ⌄
Absolutely! We invite all families for a 2-hour complimentary trial morning where your child can experience the classroom, meet teachers, and you can observe the environment firsthand. This is part of our campus visit process.
What documents are needed for registration? ⌄
Just three things: child's birth certificate (original + copy), one recent passport-size photograph of the child, and the completed registration form. We keep it simple — no stress!
Is after-school care available? ⌄
Yes! We offer after-school care from 1 PM to 6 PM with supervised free play, creative activities, and a healthy snack. This can be added to any of our programs at an additional fee of ₹12,000/annum.
Is there a transport facility? ⌄
We currently do not have school transport. However, we are happy to connect you with trusted third-party school cab services used by many of our families in the Whitefield area.
How do you communicate with parents? ⌄
We use a dedicated parent communication app for daily updates, photos, and learning highlights. Monthly written progress reports, termly parent-teacher conferences, and regular parent workshops keep you deeply connected to your child's journey.
