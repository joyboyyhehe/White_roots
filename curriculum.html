📚 Three Programs

Learning Designed for Every Stage

Each program is a distinct world of learning tailored to what children need most at that particular stage of development.

🌱 Ages 2–3 Years

Sprouting Seeds

The gentlest introduction to learning. Warm, sensory-rich experiences that help toddlers separate from home, explore freely, and build their very first friendships.

Sensory PlayMusic MovementSocial Skills Motor SkillsLanguage
🌿 Ages 3–4 Years

Little Saplings

The year of independence and imagination. Children express themselves through art, stories, dramatic play, and early mathematical thinking while building confidence.

IndependenceStorytelling Art & CraftPre-Math DramaScience Curiosity
🌳 Ages 4–5 Years

Budding Branches

The bridge to formal learning. A rich, structured program that builds literacy, numeracy, critical thinking, and all the 21st century skills children need for Grade 1.

Reading ReadyNumeracy WritingCritical Thinking CollaborationTech Literacy

📖 Curriculum Domains

What Children Learn
& How They Learn It

📖

Language & Literacy

🔤

Phonics & Letter Recognition

Systematic phonics using songs, flashcards, and hands-on letter tracing activities.

📚

Storytelling & Comprehension

Daily read-alouds with guided questions to build comprehension and narrative thinking.

✏️

Pre-Writing Skills

Fine motor exercises, pencil grip training, and pattern writing on sand, clay, and paper.

🗣️

Oral Language Development

Circle time sharing, show & tell, puppet theater, and vocabulary-building conversations.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

Letter RecognitionPhonemic AwarenessEarly ReadingVocabulary BuildingListening SkillsPre-Writing
🔢

Mathematics

🔢

Number Sense & Counting

Counting with physical objects, number songs, and one-to-one correspondence activities.

🔷

Shapes & Spatial Awareness

Identifying 2D and 3D shapes through building, sorting, and real-world shape hunts.

🎯

Patterns & Logical Sequencing

Creating, extending, and identifying patterns using colors, shapes, and everyday objects.

⚖️

Measurement & Comparison

Comparing sizes, weights, and lengths using non-standard units and hands-on measurement tools.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

Number 1–100Basic OperationsShape RecognitionPattern LogicMeasurementProblem Solving
🔬

Science & Nature

🌱

Plant Science & Gardening

Children grow their own plants, observe life cycles, and learn about nature's incredible processes.

🧪

Simple Experiments

Safe, age-appropriate experiments that build scientific thinking, observation, and wonder.

🌦️

Weather & Environment

Daily weather tracking, seasons, and developing awareness about our beautiful natural world.

🦋

Animals & Their World

Studying animals, habitats, and ecosystems through books, videos, and hands-on models.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

Scientific CuriosityObservation SkillsEnvironmental AwarenessHypothesis Forming
🎨

Creative Arts

🖌️

Visual Arts & Painting

Finger painting, watercolors, collage, and mixed media to express feelings and ideas freely.

🎭

Drama & Role Play

Dramatic play and theater that build empathy, language, emotional intelligence, and confidence.

🎵

Music & Movement

Singing, rhythm instruments, movement, and dance that build musical intelligence and coordination.

🏺

Clay & 3D Art

Sculpting with clay develops fine motor skills, spatial reasoning, and creative expression.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

Creative ConfidenceFine Motor SkillsEmotional ExpressionAesthetic Sense
🤝

Social & Emotional Skills

💬

Communication & Sharing

Learning to take turns, share, listen, and communicate needs and feelings clearly and kindly.

❤️

Empathy & Kindness

Stories, discussions, and role-play scenarios that build genuine understanding of others' feelings.

🧘

Self-Regulation

Breathing techniques, calm-down corners, and mindfulness practices for emotional balance.

🏆

Confidence & Leadership

Opportunities for each child to lead, make choices, and experience genuine accomplishment.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

Emotional RegulationEmpathyConflict ResolutionSelf-Confidence
🏃

Physical Education

🏃

Gross Motor Development

Running, jumping, climbing, and balancing activities that build strong, coordinated bodies.

🎾

Ball Games & Team Sports

Age-appropriate team activities that build cooperation, sportsmanship, and physical fitness.

🧘

Kids' Yoga & Mindfulness

Simple yoga postures, breathing, and mindfulness to build body awareness and calm minds.

💃

Dance & Rhythm

Creative movement, folk dance, and rhythm activities that combine physical and musical growth.
